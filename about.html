Welcome to Pleotek

Pleotek is an award-winning healthcare technology company.

Helping others is at the heart of Pleotek.

Each of our founding team has been moved by their personal experiences within social care to make a change.

Because of that, we are driven by a deep desire to see care be delivered more efficiently, and for those who help to be supported by powerful tools that allow them to do what they do so well - help others.Â
